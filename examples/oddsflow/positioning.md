# Positioning

## One-liner
OddsFlow — AI football predictions with a verified performance record

## Tagline
Predict smarter. Verify everything.

## Short Description
OddsFlow is an AI football prediction platform covering the Premier League, Bundesliga, Serie A, La Liga, and Ligue 1. It delivers 1x2, Asian handicap, and over/under signals — each backed by a published track record bettors can audit. Built for serious football bettors who want data-driven signals, not anonymous tips.

## Long Description
OddsFlow analyzes millions of historical matches, real-time data, and odds movement to surface high-confidence predictions across Europe's top five leagues. Every signal — whether 1x2, handicap, or over/under — comes with verified performance history so bettors can evaluate accuracy before acting. The platform tracks odds movement and market trends alongside its own model outputs, giving users both the prediction and the context behind it. No black boxes, no unverifiable claims.

## Problem
Most football prediction tools either hide their methodology or have no track record worth publishing. Bettors are left choosing between anonymous tipsters with selective memory and raw data platforms that require building their own models. There is no reliable way to know if a signal source actually performs before you've already lost money on it.

## Solution
OddsFlow publishes verified performance records for every signal type it produces — 1x2, Asian handicap, and over/under — across all five covered leagues. Its AI flags matches with the highest confidence scores and pairs predictions with odds movement analysis, so bettors can see both the model's output and how the market is moving. Transparency is structural, not optional.

## Why Now
Asian handicap and odds movement data are now available at a granularity that was inaccessible to retail bettors five years ago. AI models can process that volume of signal in real time; manual analysis cannot. Bettors who previously relied on gut feel or fragmented tipster networks now have the infrastructure to make structured, evidence-based decisions.
