# Product Hunt Draft

## Name
OddsFlow

## Tagline
AI football predictions with a verified track record

## Short Pitch
OddsFlow delivers AI-generated predictions across 1x2, Asian handicap, and over/under markets for Europe's top five leagues — ranked by confidence score. Every signal is logged against a published performance record so you can audit the model's history before you act on anything. Built for football bettors and sports traders who need accountability, not just tips.

## First Comment
Hi PH — maker here.

I built OddsFlow after getting burned too many times by tipster services that had no verifiable history. A good month looked identical to a good model, and there was no way to tell the difference until you'd already lost money on it.

OddsFlow covers the Premier League, Bundesliga, Serie A, La Liga, and Ligue 1 — generating 1x2, Asian handicap, and over/under signals for each match. Every prediction gets a confidence score, and every result gets logged. You can pull up the historical record for any signal type before you decide whether the model is worth following. The AI is analyzing odds movement and market trends in real time, not just rehashing pre-match stats.

The thing that frustrates me most about existing tools is the lack of accountability. Anyone can publish tips. Almost nobody publishes a complete, auditable record of how those tips actually performed over six months. That's the gap OddsFlow is designed to close — a platform where the track record is part of the product, not an afterthought.

Two things I'd genuinely love feedback on:
1. Which markets matter most to you — match result, handicap, or totals?
2. What would make you trust an AI prediction tool enough to actually use it?

Check it out: https://oddsflow.ai
