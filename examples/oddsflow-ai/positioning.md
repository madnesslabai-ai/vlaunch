# Positioning

## One-liner
OddsFlow — AI football predictions with a verified performance record

## Tagline
Predict smarter. Verify everything.

## Short Description
OddsFlow is an AI football prediction platform covering the Premier League, Bundesliga, Serie A, La Liga, and Ligue 1. It delivers 1x2, Asian handicap, and over/under signals ranked by confidence score — with a published track record football bettors and sports traders can audit before they act.

## Long Description
OddsFlow analyzes millions of historical matches, real-time data, and odds movement to surface structured predictions across Europe's top five leagues. Every signal comes with a confidence score and is logged against a verified performance record, so users know exactly how the model has performed over time. It covers the full range of betting markets — match result, handicap, and totals — in a single platform. Built for football bettors and sports traders who need accountable AI signals, not anonymous tips.

## Problem
Most football prediction tools publish tips without accountability — no performance history, no confidence scoring, no way to distinguish a good model from a lucky streak. Bettors and traders who want data-driven signals across multiple European leagues are forced to cross-reference scattered sources, each with its own blind spots and zero transparency about past results.

## Solution
OddsFlow tracks odds movement, generates AI predictions across 1x2, Asian handicap, and over/under markets, and publishes verified performance records for every signal type. Users can filter by confidence score to focus on the matches where the model has the highest conviction, and evaluate historical accuracy before committing to any strategy.

## Why Now
AI models can now process the volume and variety of football data — live odds, historical results, market movement — that manual analysis and traditional tipsters cannot handle at scale. As regulated betting markets expand across Europe and Asia, traders are moving toward systematic, auditable approaches. OddsFlow is built for that shift.
