## Name

## Tagline
OddsFlow — verification-first football intelligence for the top European leagues

## Short Pitch
OddsFlow is an AI-powered football intelligence platform covering the Premier League, Bundesliga, Serie A, La Liga, and Ligue 1 — with 1x2 predictions, handicap analysis, and over/under signals you can verify against a published track record. Built for football bettors and sports traders who want transparent AI signals they can verify, not gut feelings or anonymous tipsters.

## First Comment
Hi PH — maker here.

We built OddsFlow because football bettors and sports traders deserve better tools than what's currently available — and most existing options are either too generic or not designed with this audience in mind.

OddsFlow is an AI-powered football intelligence platform covering the Premier League, Bundesliga, Serie A, La Liga, and Ligue 1 — with 1x2 predictions, handicap analysis, and over/under signals you can verify against a published track record. Every signal comes with the reasoning behind it, and we publish our track record so you can judge for yourself whether the analysis holds up.

The core problem we're solving:
Football bettors, sports traders, and ai-first football fans face a fragmented landscape of prediction tools. Most lack transparency, published track records, or coverage across multiple leagues. Getting a clear, data-driven view means pulling from multiple sources and doing the analysis manually.

We're early and building in the open. Would genuinely love feedback from this community:
1. Does this solve a real problem for you?
2. What would make it more useful?

Check it out: https://oddsflow.ai
