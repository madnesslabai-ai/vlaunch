## Long Description
OddsFlow is an AI-powered football intelligence platform covering the Premier League, Bundesliga, Serie A, La Liga, and Ligue 1 — with 1x2 predictions, handicap analysis, and over/under signals you can verify against a published track record.

Football bettors and sports traders are stuck stitching together scattered stats, inconsistent tipsters, and opaque models — with no way to verify what actually works. OddsFlow addresses that by giving them a football intelligence platform that surfaces structured predictions and lets users evaluate the reasoning behind every signal.

https://oddsflow.ai

## Problem
Football bettors, sports traders, and ai-first football fans face a fragmented landscape of prediction tools. Most lack transparency, published track records, or coverage across multiple leagues. Getting a clear, data-driven view means pulling from multiple sources and doing the analysis manually.

## Solution
OddsFlow consolidates AI-driven predictions, odds analysis, and historical performance tracking into one platform. It covers the top European leagues, provides structured match-level insights, and publishes its track record so users can judge signal quality for themselves.

## Why Now
Football data has become far richer, and AI models can now process it at a scale tipsters and spreadsheets cannot. OddsFlow arrives as the gap between available data and accessible analysis is at its widest — users who want predictive intelligence, not just raw stats, finally have a realistic option.
